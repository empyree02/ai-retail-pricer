# Demo Script – AI Retail Pricer

1. **Introduction (20s)**
   - “Hi, I’m [Your Name]. Let me introduce *AI Retail Pricer*, a simple app that helps small businesses automatically compute fair retail prices.”

2. **Problem (30s)**
   - “Many SMEs don’t know how to price products — they either lose profit or overprice. This tool solves that with transparent calculations.”

3. **App Walkthrough (2min)**
   - “You enter your purchase cost and adjust overheads: logistics, IT, personnel, and margin.”
   - “The app instantly suggests a retail price.”
   - “You can also upload a CSV and get a full price list.”

4. **Impact (30s)**
   - “If widely used, small retailers could standardize pricing, improve profit consistency, and reduce guesswork.”

5. **Tools Used (30s)**
   - “Developed in Python using Streamlit. I used ChatGPT and Hugging Face to design and structure the project. The code is open and reproducible.”

6. **Closing (10s)**
   - “That’s AI Retail Pricer — smart, simple, and practical.”
